Alternative Consideration:

Violet (V) should be assigned a color first.

Reason: Violet has the highest degree (5 neighbors), making it the most constrained variable. By the Most Constrained Variable heuristic, we should assign colors to the most constrained variables first because:

  • It's most likely to cause a dead-end if left for later
  • It provides the most information to guide subsequent assignments
  • It helps detect unsolvable situations early in the search
